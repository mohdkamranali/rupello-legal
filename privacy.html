Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Introduction

Rupello ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Rupello mobile application (the "App").

By using Rupello, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account creation and authentication
  • Password - Encrypted and stored securely for account access
  • Account creation date - To track when you joined Rupello

Financial Data

You voluntarily provide:

  • Transaction details - Amounts, descriptions, categories, dates
  • Account information - Account names, types, and balances
  • Budget data - Budget limits and categories
  • Savings goals - Goal titles, target amounts, and dates
  • Custom categories - Names, icons, and colors you create

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information - Device model, operating system version
  • App usage data - Features used, crash reports, performance data
  • Log data - IP address, access times, app errors

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. Provide the Service

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Store and sync your financial data
  • Enable app functionality (budgets, goals, reports)

2. Improve the App

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

3. Communications

  • Send password reset emails (when requested)
  • Send important service updates
  • Respond to your support requests

4. Security

  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Verify your identity

Data Storage and Security

Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely on:

  • Replit's cloud infrastructure - Hosted in secure data centers
  • PostgreSQL database - Industry-standard database with encryption
  • Your device - Cached data for offline functionality

How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption - All data transmitted is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Password hashing - Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (industry standard)
  • Authentication tokens - Secure JWT tokens for session management
  • Access controls - Strict user isolation - you can only access your own data
  • Regular backups - Automated backups to prevent data loss

Data Retention

  • Active accounts - Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts - Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Backups - Backup copies deleted within 90 days

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
  • ❌ Share your financial data with advertisers
  • ❌ Use your data for marketing purposes
  • ❌ Track you across other apps or websites

We MAY share data only when:

  • You request it - When you export your own data
  • Legal requirement - If required by law or legal process
  • Service providers - With trusted providers who help run our service (Replit for hosting)
  • With your consent - Any other sharing will require your explicit permission

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

Access Your Data

  • View all your financial data within the app
  • Export your data as CSV or PDF anytime

Modify Your Data

  • Edit or delete any transaction, account, budget, or goal
  • Update your email or password
  • Create, edit, or delete custom categories

Delete Your Account

  • Request account deletion at any time
  • All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies removed within 90 days

Opt-Out

  • You can stop using the app at any time
  • Uninstalling the app does not delete your account - you must request deletion
